Practo Reach is an ad slot for clinics and hospitals for getting better visibility on Practo.com.

Here’s how your Practo Reach ad will look

Here’s how your Practo Reach ad will look Your Practo Reach ad appears on all the prime positions on Practo listing pages — desktop, mobile web and app.

Who can use Reach?

Only clinics, hospitals and medical health establishments can subscribe to Practo Reach ad slots in accordance with MCI regulation 7.12.

Note: Doctors cannot purchase Practo Reach.

Here’s how your Practo Reach ad will look

- Make your practice visible to millions of users on Practo.com
- Get instant SMS alerts on every patient call
- Showcase special offers and services of your practice

Target your audience

By locality

Choose your ad placement by selecting multiple localities closest to your practice location.

By keywords

Select keywords that are the most relevant to your practice — symptoms, services and speciality.

e.g. A clinic that specialises in diabetes treatment can choose keywords like endocrinologist, sugar disease etc.

Reach Dashboard

Track the performance of your Practo Reach subscription from anywhere (Practo website or app) through Practo Reach dashboard.

Email Updates

We’ll notify you about your Practo Reach subscription activity and number of views through weekly emails.
