Here’s how your WhealthyLife Reach ad will look Your WhealthyLife Reach ad appears on all the prime positions on WhealthyLife listing pages — desktop, mobile web and app.

Who can use Reach?

Only clinics, hospitals and medical health establishments can subscribe to WhealthyLife Reach ad slots in accordance with MCI regulation 7.12.

Note: Doctors cannot purchase WhealthyLife Reach.

Here’s how your WhealthyLife Reach ad will look

- Make your practice visible to millions of users on whealthylife.in
- Get instant SMS alerts on every patient call
- Showcase special offers and services of your practice

Target your audience

By locality

Choose your ad placement by selecting multiple localities closest to your practice location.

By keywords

Select keywords that are the most relevant to your practice — symptoms, services and speciality.

e.g. A clinic that specialises in diabetes treatment can choose keywords like endocrinologist, sugar disease etc.
